Shankar Mahadevan

Shankar Mahadevan was born on March 3, 1967 in Chembur, Bombay, Maharashtra, India. He is a music artist and composer, known for Kal Ho Naa Ho (2003), My Name Is Khan (2010) and Dil Chahta Hai (2001).

Pritam Chakraborty

Pritam Chakraborty, also known mononymously as Pritam, is an Indian composer, instrumentalist, guitarist, and singer. He debuted as a co-composer in the 2001 Hindi film Tere Liye. he has composed music for more than 125 Bollywood Movies.

Sanchit Balhara

Sanchit Balhara started his career as a solo composer and became best known for composing the score of Bajirao Mastani. Sanchit's elder brother, Ankit Balhara, joined him in 2018, and the duo have been composing together since Manikarnika: The Queen of Jhansi.

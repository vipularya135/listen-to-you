Ilaiyaraaja

Ilaiyaraaja is an Indian musician, composer, popular for his works in Tamil and Telugu films. Reputed to be one of the most prolific Indian composers, in a career spanning over forty-five years, he has composed over 7,000 songs and provided film scores for over 1,000 films, apart from performing in over 20,000 concerts

Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander, also credited mononymously as Anirudh, is an Indian music composer, music producer, singer who works in the Indian film industry, primarily in Tamil films. He has also composed music for various Telugu films.
